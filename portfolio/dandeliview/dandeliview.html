About

Dandeli is located at the banks of River Kali and offers breathtaking views, activites, houses a variety flora and fauna and is the best place for Adventurer's / Photographer's / Family Trips / Group Outing.

Dandeli is recognized among the most-favoured tourism destinations of India is located in Uttara Kannada district of Karnataka, India. View on Google

Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary (Kali Tiger Reserve), is home to Tigers, Leopards, Black Panthers, Elephants, Gaur, Deer, Antelopes, Bears, Peacocks and more..
